# CivicGraph

Tagline: The governance network behind Canada's public funding.

What it does: Lets anyone search a person's name and see every Canadian charity board they serve on, plus every dollar of federal and Alberta public funding flowing to those boards.

Why it matters: 23 million rows of open government data exist across CRA T3010 charity filings, federal grant disclosures, and Alberta grant records — but no public tool makes the cross-cutting governance network queryable.

Position: We surface concentration, not accusations. Every fact in the UI is sourceable to a public filing. CivicGraph is a prioritization aid for human investigators (journalists, auditors, policy researchers), not a verdict engine.

Built for: Agency 2026 Ottawa hackathon, April 29 2026.

Demo deployed in: us-west-2 (Workshop Studio sandbox).

Production target: ca-central-1 with Amazon Neptune.
